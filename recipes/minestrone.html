Minestrone

This minestrone soup recipe features vegetables, beans, and pasta in a rich, tomato-based broth. Serve this hearty soup with lightly toasted bread to mop up every last drop. The recipe can easily be doubled.

Ingredients

    ¾ cup chopped onion ½ cup chopped carrots ¼ cup chopped celery 1 (32 ounce) container chicken broth 1 (19 ounce) can cannellini beans 1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes 1 ½ cups cubed potatoes ½ cup shredded cabbage 2 tablespoons dried parsley 1 tablespoon tomato paste 2 cloves garlic, minced c1 teaspoon salt ½ cup elbow macaroni ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
